My Favorite Hip-Hop Albums of the 2020s!

I love hip-hop so much. It's hard to explain but the genre is such a vast mental playground for music nerds like me. Also, you better agree with my list (though this is more of favorites list rather than what I think are the best).

  1. Melt My Eyez See Your Future - Denzel Curry

    Far from a perfect album but the highs are just so high. This is my go-to record to spin on the turntable on rainy days. "Walkin'" is my favorite song off this project. Very soulful album for a rapper known for their aggressive style. Beautiful album with heavy introspective elements.

  2. Chromakopia - Tyler, The Creator

    Chromakopia holds a lot of sentimental value for me. The most introspective project of one of this generation's most polarizing rap artists. "Darling, I" is one of my favorite songs of all time. Also got to attend the Chromakopia tour! Overall a very meaningful album.

  3. SCARING THE HOES - JPEGMAFIA & Danny Brown

    Absolute weirdo neurodivergent energy bouncing back and forth between Peggy and Danny. I have no clue what they're saying on this album, but damn the beats are unmatched. The sampling on this one isn't talked about enough. Hard to listen to this album without at least standing up.

  4. Let God Sort Em Out - Clipse

    The first album I have ever listened to the first minute after its release. Their rapping styles are so simple compared to a lot of rappers today yet they hold on to your attention like insane. Of course, we can't talk about LGSEO without praising Pharrell's stupidly killer production.

  5. The Forever Story - JID

    My favorite album, at least for the 2020s. Such a heavy, substantial project that does not get the recognition it deserves outside of its hit single "Surround Sound," but understandably so because that song is a banger. Super dense even for an project with a playtime of just above 1 hour. JID raps (and sings) like crazy. Check it out!
